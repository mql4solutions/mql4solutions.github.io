Business

MQL4Solutions(SM), Sydney, Australia is a bespoke MT4 development shop that brings a professional Development and QA process to retail traders.

Each Order follows a minimalized Waterfall Cycle passing through Technical Specifications, Development and QA Stages before Delivery. This is a minimalized industrial software engineering process.

Price and delivery time quotes are pre-calculated (project-based, per Waterfall Cycle). Project-based pricing offers customers a way of valuing quality and functional outcomes, rather than valuing by a vague metric such as development time. MQL4Solutions pricing structure values efficient development and a clear and fast service with managed expected outcomes.

When you Order, you communicate requirements to a Technical Analyst who will support the materialization of your outcomes. However, you must own all strategic and calculative aspects of your request.

MQL4Solutions is a boutique outlet founded by IT engineering accredited professionals who work in the Financial Markets sector, which mainly deals with development within the Stocks and Futures derivative markets.

We document and grow systems born with MQL4Solutions; we do not provide support for external code. Products delivered by MQL4Solutions contain code entirely authored by MQL4Solutions lead development; we do not outsource.

MQL4Solutions provides high value for clients requiring high quality, robust and reliable engineering.
