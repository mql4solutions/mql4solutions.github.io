About Us

Business

MQL4Solutions, Sydney, Australia is a bespoke MT4 development house that brings a professional Development and QA process to retail traders.

Each order follows a minimalized waterfall cycle passing through Specifications, Development and QA with managed expectations, tracked documentation, and clean software engineering practices.

When you order, you communicate your real-time trading steps. Using our in-house engineering, we develop MT4 Indicators and Experts so as to deliver you an automation of your trading steps, and therefore deliver the various benefits of having a robot that can automate your trading day or be back-tested. However, you must communicate all aspects of your trading steps objectively for automation. We document, maintain and track clients' system specifications for long-term support.

MQL4Solutions is a boutique founded by IT engineering professionals who work in the financial markets sector, which mainly deals with development of real-time mission critical systems for stocks, futures and derivatives. Our role is in system engineering, not in financial consulting.

We document and grow systems born with MQL4Solutions in-house engineering; we do not provide ad-hoc dev-support for outside code.

MQL4Solutions provides high value for clients requiring high quality, robust and reliable engineering for automating their trading day in MT4.
