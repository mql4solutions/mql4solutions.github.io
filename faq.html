Frequently Asked Questions

Service • Technical • Pricing • Privacy and Copyright

Service

Can I add to the Specifications after the Gig has started?

Once you buy, we are in the Development Stage. To start another Specifications-Development-QA Cycle, you can buy a Revision (or new Gig, recommended) where you can Specify new requirements and get a quote for your evolving Specifications. Also see [Definition of 1 Gig] below.

 

Definition of 1 Gig/Cycle/Purchase - "I want to test my strategy"

For 1 Gig, we do not do FREE revisions just because you change strategy after you try your Indicator/EA. Strategies will change all the time - that's trading. If we already did what you Specified, please Mark Complete and Review the work. Then order a new Gig for your new request so we can code it.

 

Can you modify, enhance or fix a bug in my code that has been developed externally?

MQL4Solutions does not maintain or take up the support of projects that have been started or altered externally. We keep documentation on projects started at MQL4Solutions and support extensions of these systems only, due to a large overhead in refactoring poorly designed code embedded with bugs.

 

Can you decompile an EX4?

We do not provide any kind of Dev-support for systems developed or modified externally, including decompilation or machine/assembly code modification; therefore we cannot support such requests.

 

Can you make an Indicator or Expert "just like this one"?

We cannot analyze or strategize for you. We will not read through articles or watch tutorial videos and make conclusions for you. You must do the analyses yourself, then specify your strategic steps precisely enough to be used between us as a contract of software output correctness (Specification).

 

Technical

I want a Custom Sound AND a Popup for the Alert

MT4 Popups use the global Alert sound configured under Options - Events - Alert. Global sounds override custom sounds in MT4, so your custom wav file will not be audible if a Popup occurs at the same time.

 

Pricing

Can I have a discount?

We have set pricing. We recommend that successful customers should have breathing space in their budget to purchase version enhancements based on evolving requirements and discoveries. In general, our bespoke development front is a boutique shop, not a large scale labor service. Customers seeking lowest-end budget labor should certainly seek an alternative vendor. There would be little benefit in beginning a relationship of diminishing value for both the Customer and MQL4Solutions in the short or long term.

 

Privacy and Copyright

Can you sign an NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement)?

MQL4Solutions takes legal contracts very seriously. In most cases, we will not sign an NDA if we believe the customer will discuss a non-unique topic, resulting in the contract restricting us from operating our business normally on topics that are widely discussed. Software authorship and copyright are complex topics; for example, J. Welles Wilder Jr. is the father of the RSI technical indicator mathematical formula, but the inbuilt MT4 RSI indicator is a materialization of RSI in software authored by MetaQuotes and therefore this particular piece of code belongs to MetaQuotes. A system powered by an MQL4Solutions engine contains code infrastructure for reliable execution that is not the intellectual property of the customer and cannot be claimed as such. We do not recommend going into a legal discussion. We recommend that customers who believe they own a unique concept should obtain a patent.
