Order Process

We model each order as a minimalized waterfall software project cycle. For best project outcomes, Keep It Simple and order enhancements later.

1 Are We Right for You?

We code Indicators and Experts in MT4 for real traders requiring robust, modular, reliable and extensible systems.

You're an IB or Trader with a clear trading recipe.

We're engineers who build it with extensibility, stability and user-friendliness.

You're ready if:

  • Your rules are quantitatively specific, such that if 1000 people follow your steps in real-time, all 1000 people would execute identically due to how specific and clear your rules are.

You're not ready if:

  • Your rules are actually vague guidelines or theories like:
    "where there's resistance..."
    "where it's trending... ".
  • Your rules are actually just retrospective wishlists like:
    "I want an EA that Buys every time the market makes a low".


Our recommendation: Use off-the-shelf indicators, practice writing down trading steps and then testing these steps in real-time honestly without adding human judgement when executing. Come back when when you're ready to present these steps for automation.

2 Send Us Your Requirements

Be specific. Keep It Simple. We'll quote price and delivery time, establish clear Specifications and send a Gig Offer.

Order on Fiverr

3 We Develop and Deliver

Once you accept the Gig Offer, we move the final Specifications into Development and will deliver as Specified before the countdown ends.

4 Enhance and Extend

Extend: After v.1.0 is delviered, come back anytime to order an extension or change. We repeat the Order Process cycle above, assessing Specifications and quoting for the enhancement to v.2.0.

Success Rule: Keep It Simple & Extend

MQL4Solutions focusses on long term relationships with clients. We grow systems modularly with frequent 'Agile' deliveries and shorter, clearly specified Gig cycles.

This means frequent deliveries, a well-specified modular system, and the ability to use the system early and re-assess strategic direction before adding bells and whistles.

Our most successful clients develop a gigantic hybrid system over time, containing thousands of possible strategy trajectory combinations and management switches using our highly extensible and modular development methods.

MQL4Solutions supports long-term relationships for system growth, not one-off grabs, so we will not take orders that include too many all-in buzz-word features just dumped at us without commitment to explaining all parts of the strategy. If you are ordering, you must have a deeply detailed understanding of your strategy, enough to answer some very detailed questions that we will ask you about your steps.
